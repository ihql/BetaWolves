HOME OF THE BETAWOLVES!

Based in Carney, MI


The BetaWolves (a.k.a. Team 6637) is an FRC, FTC and FLL robotics team based
in Carney, Michigan in the northern Upper Peninsula.

Our team is known to thrive throughout the robotics fields.
The BetaWolves made it's first appearence in 2017, where we won
the Highest Rookie Seed and the Rookie Inspiration Award!
