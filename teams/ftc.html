THE BETAWOLVES FTC TEAM!

Based in Carney, MI


The BetaWolves FTC (a.k.a. Team 19679) is an FTC robotics team based
in Carney, Michigan in the northern Upper Peninsula.

We are a team of young, strong minds interested in the robotics field.
The BetaWolves FTC made it's first appearence in 2021, where we won
the Finalist Alliance - Captain Award at the MI Kingsford FTC Qualifier 2021!

We recently won the Judges' Choice Award at the MI Kingsford FTC Qualifier 2022!
